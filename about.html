Al-Nima Global Health Services is a healthcare facilitator that connects patients with 23 partner hospitals across Delhi, Gurugram, and Noida. We assist with appointment scheduling, hospital selection, and provide 24/7 patient support across 27 medical specialties including cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, oncology, and more.

You can book an appointment by filling out our online appointment form on the website, selecting your preferred hospital, department, date, and time. Alternatively, you can contact us directly at +91 92171 50972 or email us at alnimaglobalhealthservices@gmail.com. Our team will confirm your appointment and provide all necessary details.

We have partnerships with 23 leading hospitals across three major cities in the National Capital Region: 8 hospitals in New Delhi (including Apollo, BLK-Max, Fortis Escorts), 12 hospitals in Gurugram (including Medanta, Artemis, Fortis Memorial Research Institute), and 3 hospitals in Noida (including Fortis, Jaypee, and Yatharth Super Speciality Hospital).

Yes, we specialize in assisting international patients seeking medical treatment in India. We provide comprehensive support including hospital selection, appointment coordination, travel assistance, accommodation recommendations, and language interpretation services. Our team ensures a smooth healthcare journey from your home country to India.

For medical emergencies, please call our 24/7 emergency helpline at +91 92171 50972. Our team will immediately connect you with the nearest partner hospital's emergency department and coordinate urgent care. We provide round-the-clock support to ensure you receive timely medical attention.

Our appointment booking and hospital coordination services are provided to help patients access quality healthcare. For detailed information about service fees and what's included in our support packages, please contact us directly at alnimaglobalhealthservices@gmail.com or call +91 92171 50972.
