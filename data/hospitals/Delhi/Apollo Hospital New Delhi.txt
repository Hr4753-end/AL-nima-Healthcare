Apollo Hospital in New Delhi is a flagship facility of the Apollo Hospitals Group, one of Asia’s
leading integrated healthcare providers. Known for its high-quality care, the hospital offers a
comprehensive range of medical services while maintaining international standards in
healthcare excellence.
About the Hospital
●​ The hospital boasts state-of-the-art medical technology, including advanced imaging
systems, robotic surgery units, and tools for minimally invasive procedures. These
innovations allow for precise diagnostics and highly effective treatments across various
specialties. Established in 1983 by Dr. Prathap C. Reddy, Apollo Hospitals was
instrumental in introducing the corporate healthcare model to India, playing a pivotal role
in modernizing and expanding private healthcare across the nation.
●​ Apollo Hospitals has grown into a premier integrated healthcare provider in Asia, with a
vast network that includes hospitals, pharmacies, primary care and diagnostic clinics, as
well as various retail health services. The Group extends its reach globally with
telemedicine services, health insurance solutions, consultancy, and educational
programs through Medvarsity, offering courses in nursing, hospital management, and
more. Complementing its clinical services are initiatives like ‘ASK Apollo’ for online
consultations and Apollo Home Health for continuous care, ensuring a holistic approach
to patient well-being and healthcare accessibility.
●​ Apollo Hospitals’ legacy is defined by a commitment to clinical excellence, affordable
care, and the integration of cutting-edge technology. As a trailblazer in healthcare, Apollo
has consistently introduced advanced medical technologies to India, setting new
benchmarks in patient care. Notably, the Apollo Proton Cancer Centre in Chennai
represents a major milestone, being the first of its kind in Southeast Asia. This facility
offers state-of-the-art proton therapy, solidifying Apollo's leadership in specialized cancer
treatment and reinforcing its mission to provide world-class healthcare solutions.

Team & Specialities
Apollo Hospital, New Delhi, stands as a hub for specialized care in fields such as cardiology,
oncology, neurology, orthopedics, gastroenterology, and organ transplantation. The hospital’s
dedicated centers are equipped with cutting-edge technology and are managed by top
specialists who collaborate within multidisciplinary teams. With a strong emphasis on
patient-centric care, Apollo Hospital ensures that each patient receives a personalized treatment
plan tailored to their unique medical requirements, reinforcing its commitment to delivering the
best healthcare solutions.
The specialized centers at Apollo Hospital are focused on providing advanced healthcare
