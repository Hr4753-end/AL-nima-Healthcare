Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI), established in 2001, is renowned as a premier
multi-super specialty quaternary care hospital, distinguished for its clinical excellence and
advanced technology. As the flagship institution of Fortis Healthcare, one of India's leading
healthcare organizations, FMRI features a team of esteemed clinicians, superspecialists, and
expert nurses committed to providing outstanding patient care.
About the Hospital
●​ FMRI holds prestigious accreditations from JCI, NABH, and NABL, underscoring its
dedication to superior quality and patient care. In 2023, the hospital was recognized for
its exceptional Bone Marrow Transplant services at the Bangladesh International
Healthcare Expo and received accolades at the Advantage Healthcare India Medical
Value Travel Awards. Additionally, FMRI was honored with a Certificate of Appreciation
from the Ministry of Health of the Republic of South Sudan, further affirming its global
recognition and commitment to excellence.
●​ Experience excellence at our advanced Centers of Excellence in Robotic Surgery,
Oncology (covering Medical, Surgical, Gynecological, and Radiation specialties),
Cardiology, Cardiac Surgery, Transplants (Kidney and Liver), Hematology, Hemato
Oncology, Bone Marrow Transplant, Gastroenterology, GI Surgery, Neurology,
Neurosurgery, Spine Surgery, Urology, Orthopedics, Joint Replacement & Sports
Medicine, Minimal Access & Bariatric Surgery, and Pediatrics.
●​ FMRI has garnered notable acclaim for its exceptional services. In 2021, it was honored
as the Best Hospital in North India for Oncology, Cardiology, and Gastroenterology by
the Economic Times Healthcare Awards. Additionally, it earned international recognition
by being the only Indian hospital listed in Newsweek’s Top 25 ‘World’s Best Smart
Hospitals 2021’.

Team & Specialities
●​ Fortis Hospital, Gurugram, stands out as a leader in Neurosciences, Oncology, Renal
Sciences, Orthopaedics, Cardiac Sciences, and Obstetrics and Gynaecology, solidifying
its reputation as one of the premier hospitals in the city.
●​ The hospital boasts state-of-the-art Centres of Excellence across multiple specialties,
including Robotic Surgery, Oncology (Medical, Surgical, Gynecological, and Radiation),
Cardiology and Cardiac Surgery, Kidney and Liver Transplants, Haematology and
Haemato-Oncology, Bone Marrow Transplant, Gastroenterology and GI Surgery,
Neurology and Neuro-Spine Surgery, Urology, Orthopaedics, Joint Replacement and
Sports Medicine, Minimal Access and Bariatric Surgery, and Paediatrics.
●​ Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI) is at the forefront of advanced
super-specialization in oncology, trauma, and paediatric care, with embedded excellence
in neurosciences, minimal access surgery, cardiac sciences, and orthopaedics. Notably,
FMRI was the first hospital in India to perform a Swap Liver Transplant. The hospital also
offers a range of advanced procedures, including Lateral Internal Sphincterotomy,
Endoscopic Sleeve Gastroplasty, Haemorrhoidal Artery Ligation and Recto-Anal Repair
(HAL-RAR), Total Thyroidectomy, and Laparoscopy.
Infrastructure
●​ Fortis Memorial Research Institute (FMRI) spans 150,000 square feet of eco-friendly
space, featuring a range of advanced amenities designed for superior medical care. As a
310-bed facility, FMRI is equipped with 15 operating theaters, 105 ICU beds, and
specialized areas for robotic surgeries.
●​ The hospital is renowned for its cutting-edge medical technology, including modular
operating rooms and specialized facilities for diverse medical procedures. Notably, FMRI
houses India’s first “see-through” glass lab, enabling immediate collection and
processing of blood samples.
●​ Among its advanced imaging technologies, FMRI features the latest 256 Slice Brilliance
iCT Scanner, capable of capturing a complete image of the heart in just two heartbeats.

It also offers a Bi-Plane Cath Lab and an ultra-modern Cardiac Cath Lab for
comprehensive cardiac and vascular interventions.
●​ For neurological procedures, FMRI offers a specialized CT-based Brain Suite with
intraoperative CT capabilities, enhancing precision during surgeries. The facility utilizes
Gamma Knife technology for precise, non-invasive treatments, and is equipped with the
world’s first 3 Tesla Digital MRI technology, advancing capabilities in advanced radiation
therapy.
●​ Patient comfort is a priority, with Wi-Fi-enabled rooms, personalized meal plans, and an
exclusive lounge designed for international patients.
