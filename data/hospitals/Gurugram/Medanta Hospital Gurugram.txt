About the Hospital
●​ Medanta Hospital, established in 2009 by the distinguished Dr. Naresh Trehan, a leading
figure in cardiovascular and cardiothoracic surgery, was founded with a mission to
provide world-class medical care. Combining state-of-the-art technology with a focus on
compassionate care, Medanta reflects Dr. Trehan's dedication to elevating healthcare in
India.
●​ The hospital holds both NABH and NABL accreditations, affirming its commitment to the
highest quality standards and patient safety protocols. These prestigious certifications
highlight Medanta's adherence to strict guidelines, ensuring that patients receive
exceptional care in every aspect.
●​ Medanta's advanced infrastructure, paired with its highly skilled medical professionals,
places it among the top healthcare providers. It features several Centers of Excellence,
including the Heart Institute, Neurosciences Institute, Bone & Joint Institute, Kidney &
Urology Institute, Cancer Institute, and the Division of Medical Oncology and
Hematology. Each center is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by
specialists, ensuring comprehensive and specialized treatment for a wide range of
medical conditions.

Team & Specialities
●​ In January 2013, Medanta made history by completing India's first successful intestinal
transplant under the leadership of Dr. A.S. Soin. This landmark surgery demonstrated
the hospital's pioneering approach and commitment to pushing the frontiers of medical
science.
●​ Medanta's surgical expertise is unmatched, having successfully completed over 15,000
cardiac surgeries and 2,500 joint replacements. The hospital is also a leader in liver
transplants, with more than 500 living donor procedures, positioning it as the top center
in India and second globally. These milestones reflect Medanta’s unwavering dedication
to performing complex surgeries with precision and care.
●​ The hospital also achieved a world record for completing 30 Total Knee Replacements in
a single day, exemplifying its capacity to handle complex, high-volume operations with
remarkable efficiency.
●​ Medanta serves as a certified International Training Centre by the American Heart
Association, providing top-tier education for doctors, nurses, and paramedical staff. Its
training programs, supported by advanced simulation labs and digital resources,
empower healthcare professionals to elevate their skills under the guidance of
experienced faculty, ensuring that Medanta continues to lead in medical education.
Infrastructure
●​ Medanta Hospital, spanning across 43 acres, boasts advanced facilities designed to
meet a wide range of medical requirements. With 45 state-of-the-art operation theatres
and 1,250 beds, including over 350 critical care beds across 20 specialized
departments, Medanta is equipped to deliver comprehensive care to a large patient base
simultaneously.
●​ Alongside its extensive inpatient capacity, Medanta ensures efficient and timely care with
20 treatment beds in constant operation, enabling it to handle both routine and
emergency cases with precision. This infrastructure supports the hospital’s commitment
to high-quality patient care across a variety of needs.
●​ In 2013, Medanta further revolutionized emergency medical services by launching
India's first air ambulance service, ‘Flying Doctors India.’ This initiative reflects Medanta's
forward-thinking approach to healthcare, ensuring rapid and accessible medical
intervention for critically ill patients, regardless of their location.
