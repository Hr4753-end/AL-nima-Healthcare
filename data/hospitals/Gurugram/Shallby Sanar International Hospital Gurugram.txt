Infrastructure
●​ Sanar International Hospital, a premier 150-bed facility with 35 ICU beds dedicated to
transplant care, is strategically situated in the heart of Gurugram. The hospital upholds
global standards and a commitment to excellence in healthcare.
●​ The hospital includes a 7-bed dialysis unit, 14 specialized BMT rooms, 4 state-of-the-art
modular operating theaters, and an Endoscopy and Bronchoscopy suite. It also offers a
24-hour blood bank service for comprehensive patient care.
●​ The hospital features advanced laboratories in Radiology, Biochemistry, Hematology,
Microbiology, Histopathology, Cytology, and Clinical Pathology, providing a wide range of
diagnostic tests to ensure precise diagnosis and effective treatment.
